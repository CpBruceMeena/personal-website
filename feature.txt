# Homepage Redesign Guide - Clean & Modern Approach

## Current Issues
- **Information overload** - too much text on homepage
- **Poor visual hierarchy** - everything looks equally important
- **No clear call-to-actions** - visitors don't know what to do next
- **Lacks visual appeal** - mostly text-based with minimal visual elements
- **No compelling hook** - doesn't grab attention immediately

## New Homepage Strategy: "Less is More"

### Core Principle
The homepage should answer three questions in 10 seconds:
1. **Who are you?**
2. **What do you do?**
3. **Why should I care?**

Everything else should be accessible through clear navigation and engaging CTAs.


## Performance Optimization

### Image Optimization:
- **WebP format** for better compression
- **Lazy loading** for below-fold content
- **Responsive images** with srcset
- **Optimized dimensions** (hero photo: 400x400px max)

### Loading Strategy:
- **Critical CSS inline** for above-fold content
- **Defer non-critical JavaScript**
- **Preload key resources** (fonts, hero image)
- **Minimize HTTP requests**

## Final Recommendations

1. **Start with content** - Write compelling copy before designing
2. **Test early and often** - Get feedback from 5-10 people in your network
3. **Focus on one clear goal** - What do you want visitors to do?
4. **Keep iterating** - Launch quickly, then improve based on data
5. **Think like your audience** - Hiring managers want to see impact, not just skills

Remember: Your homepage is a landing page, not a resume. Its job is to intrigue visitors enough to explore further or contact you directly.
